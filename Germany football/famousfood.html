Famous Food

Lebanese cuisine is famous worldwide. Here are some must-try dishes:

  • Tabbouleh – Fresh herb salad
  • Fattoush – Crispy bread salad
  • Kibbeh – Ground meat and bulgur, a national dish
  • Hummus – Chickpea dip with tahini
  • Manakish – Lebanese flatbread with za'atar or cheese
  • Shawarma – Marinated, spit-roasted meat
  • Baklava – Sweet pastry with nuts and syrup
