Top 10 Places to Visit in Lebanon

  • Baalbek – The Roman temples
  • Byblos – One of the oldest cities in the world
  • Jeita Grotto – Stunning natural caves
  • Beirut – The vibrant capital
  • Cedars of God – Ancient cedar trees
  • Qadisha Valley – UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Sidon – Coastal city with a historic souk
  • Tyre – Ancient Phoenician city
  • Batroun – Coastal town known for its nightlife and beaches
  • Tripoli – Rich in Mamluk architecture
